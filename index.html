06 Intel

Frequently Asked Questions

How do I participate? +
Follow Stash Hunt on social media and sign up for hunt alerts. When a hunt goes live, we release clues publicly. Use them to track down the hidden token anywhere in the announced city zone. It's free to play — no purchase, no app, no entry fee.
Where are tokens hidden? +
Tokens are always hidden in publicly accessible, legal, and safe locations — parks, plazas, sidewalks, murals, and landmarks. Never on private property, never anywhere dangerous, and never anywhere that requires digging, climbing, or trespassing. If a clue seems to point somewhere off-limits, you're reading it wrong.
How do prizes get redeemed? +
Every winning token is verified and redeemed at our official redemption center, Trap House Cannabis on Havana. Bring the token in, staff verify it against the official hunt registry, and your prize is released on the spot. You must be 21+ with valid ID to redeem. If you're up for it, we film the reveal for the Winners Gallery.
How can businesses partner? +
Local businesses can sponsor a hunt, contribute a prize, or become an official redemption location. Partners get featured placement in clues, social content, and winner reveal videos. Head to our Partner Program page or reach out directly and we'll send the partnership deck.
What are the rules? +
Play safe, play legal, play fair. Respect public and private property, obey all traffic laws, and don't disturb businesses or residents while searching. One token per hunt, first verified find wins. Anyone can hunt, but you must be 21+ with valid ID to redeem a prize at the redemption center. Full official rules are posted with every hunt announcement.
